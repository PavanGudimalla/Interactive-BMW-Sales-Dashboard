# 🚗 BMW Global Sales & Market Share Power BI Dashboard (2010-2024)

## 🎯 Executive Summary
This project delivers a comprehensive, interactive **Power BI** dashboard designed to visualize global sales performance, pricing strategies, and market share for BMW across a 14-year dataset. By transforming raw historical sales data into an interactive decision-support tool, this project enables stakeholders to quickly isolate trends, understand regional consumer preferences, and track the adoption of emerging vehicle classes (e.g., hybrid/electric) with a few clicks.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Core Technologies:** Power BI, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), Power Query, Advanced Excel.
* **Data Processing (ETL):** Engineered robust ETL workflows using **Power Query** to clean, filter, and aggregate multi-dimensional data (Region, Year, Fuel Type, Transmission, Model, Volume, Price). 
* **Data Modeling:** Developed complex **DAX measures** to calculate dynamic market share, year-over-year growth, and weighted average pricing.
* **UI/UX Design:** Architected an intuitive front-end interface utilizing dynamic slicers, drill-down capabilities, and seamless cross-filtering to enable real-time data exploration.

## 📊 Dashboard Features & Analytical Capabilities
* **Price vs. Volume Elasticity (Viz 1 & 5):** Dual-axis column/line charts and dynamically scaled scatter plots to identify the relationship between average pricing and sales volume across different models.
* **Market Penetration (Viz 2):** Interactive donut/pie charts detailing model-specific market share within selected regions—crucial for competitive analysis.
* **Regional & Demographic Preferences (Viz 3 & 4):** Clustered bar charts and matrix heatmaps tracking the shift in consumer demand for specific transmissions and fuel types (e.g., the rise of EV/Hybrids) over time.

## 💡 Translation to Marketing & Media Planning
The architecture of this Power BI dashboard directly translates to **Marketing Analytics and Media Planning**. The ability to segment audiences dynamically (by region, product preference, and timeline using slicers) and visualize market share is identical to analyzing campaign reach, audience penetration, and demographic ad performance. This tool demonstrates a high-level capability to build automated reporting systems that empower marketing teams to allocate budgets based on real-time regional performance.
